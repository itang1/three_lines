## UX and accessibility

- **Home page is very sparse.** Title plus two buttons. It does not show what a
  filled-in notebook looks like or explain the value before asking the reader to
  open the notebook. Consider a small static example (the John 1 sample from the
  instructions page) on the landing page.
## Feature ideas

- **Highlight or bookmark passages** without writing a full note, for marking
  things to return to.
- **Reading progress per book** (which chapters have notes) is tracked for the
  sidebar dots already; surfacing a book-level progress indicator on the book
  picker would reinforce momentum.
- **Reply notifications in-app**, not just email, so signed-in users see when
  someone replied to their note.
